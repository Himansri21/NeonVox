Privacy Policy

Effective date:

What we process

Text you submit is sent to our server solely to generate audio. We do not build user profiles.

Retention

Input text and generated audio are kept only in memory long enough to produce your download. We do not persist them to disk or a database, except transient temp files during processing which are deleted automatically.

Third‑party services

If you choose the gTTS engine, text is sent to Google Text‑to‑Speech to create audio. Offline engine (pyttsx3) does not send text to third parties.

Logs

We keep minimal server logs (timestamp, success/error, engine used). We do not log the text contents.

Security

Transport is protected via HTTPS. We enforce request size limits and input validation to reduce abuse.

Your rights

Contact us to inquire about data practices or to report an issue: owner@example.com.

Copyright

Use only text you have the right to synthesize. Site visuals are custom CSS/SVG without third‑party images to avoid copyright concerns.

This policy may evolve as features change.
